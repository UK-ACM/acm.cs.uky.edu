Welcome to the University of Kentucky's chapter of
the Association of Computing Machinery Women!


who we are


We are a group of Computer Science/Engineering and Information Technology students and hobbyists. At ACM-W, we are all about creating new projects, cultivating lasting friendships, and sharing ideas with each other.

what we do


Since inception, our focus has been empowering others, particularly women, through technology. Our members have done everything from running coding workshops at local schools to building apps that help feed the homeless and connect students.
